# Qumetriq Homepage Blueprint

## Purpose

The homepage is not designed to sell a single product.

Its purpose is to introduce Qumetriq as a professional software company that builds tools for traders and investors.

Every section must reinforce trust, precision and professionalism.

---

# Primary Goal

Help visitors understand:

- Who we are.
- What we build.
- Why our approach is different.
- Why they should explore our products.

---

# Target Audience

Professional traders.

Retail traders.

Funded traders.

Algorithmic traders.

Investors interested in improving their workflow.

---

# Core Brand Values

- Precision
- Professionalism
- Simplicity
- Data-driven decisions
- Reliability
- Efficiency
- Continuous improvement

---

# Homepage Structure

1. Hero
2. The Problem
3. What We Build
4. Why Qumetriq
5. Featured Products
6. Final Call to Action
7. Footer
