# Qumetriq Design Language

## Purpose

The design language defines how every Qumetriq product should look, feel and communicate.

It exists to ensure consistency across the website, desktop applications, trading tools, documentation and future products.

---

# Design Philosophy

We don't design for decoration.

We design for clarity.

Every interface should communicate professionalism, precision and confidence.

Nothing exists without purpose.

---

# Core Principles

## Precision

Every pixel has a purpose.

Nothing is placed randomly.

---

## Simplicity

Complex problems deserve simple interfaces.

Not simplistic.

Simple.

---

## Professionalism

Every product should feel like professional software rather than consumer software.

---

## Consistency

Every Qumetriq product belongs to the same ecosystem.

Users should instantly recognize the brand without seeing the logo.

---

## White Space

Space is part of the interface.

Never fill empty space just because it exists.

---

## Motion

Animations should guide attention.

Never distract.

---

## Color

Accent colors communicate information.

Never decoration.

---

## Typography

Typography is the primary visual element.

Large titles.

Comfortable reading.

Clear hierarchy.

---

## Components

Every reusable component must:

- solve one problem
- have one responsibility
- be reusable
- be consistent
